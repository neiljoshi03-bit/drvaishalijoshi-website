BIOGRAPHY

A Distinguished Career in Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Dr. Vaishali Joshi is Mumbai's most internationally experienced Senior Obstetrician & Gynaecologist, with 24 years of practice spanning Mumbai and London. She completed her Specialist Training Programme in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at the London Deanery, achieving CCT — the gold standard UK qualification — and is registered on the Specialist Register of the General Medical Council, United Kingdom.

In 2017, Dr. Joshi was bestowed the Fellowship of the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists (FRCOG) by the Royal College, London — one of the highest international honours in her specialty, awarded in recognition of 14 years of contribution to the field at an international level.

She holds a European University Diploma in Gynaecological Operative Endoscopy from the University of Auvergne, Clermont-Ferrand, France, and has received BSCCP accreditation as a certified Colposcopist for the diagnosis and treatment of pre-cancerous lesions. She has also trained with the da Vinci Robotic Surgical System at the Chitra Sethia MAS Centre, University College London.

Dr. Joshi completed her MBBS and MD in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at Seth G.S. Medical College and King Edward Memorial Hospital, Mumbai — two of India's most respected medical institutions. After completing her postgraduate training in Obstetrics & Gynaecology from KEM Hospital, Mumbai, Dr. Joshi successfully completed a rigorous Specialist Training Programme in the United Kingdom, spanning 14 years of specialist practice across leading NHS teaching hospitals.

Her practice attracts referrals from across India and internationally, including patients presenting for specialist care in high-risk obstetrics, complex gynaecological surgery and management of pre-cancerous conditions. Outside of clinical practice, Dr. Joshi contributes to the advancement of the specialty through peer-reviewed publications, the mentorship of postgraduate trainees and by presenting at leading conferences across India and internationally. She is recognised by colleagues and patients alike as the definitive choice for obstetric and gynaecological care in Mumbai.
