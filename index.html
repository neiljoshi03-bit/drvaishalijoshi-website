Dr. Vaishali Joshi is Mumbai's most internationally experienced Senior Obstetrician & Gynaecologist, with 24 years of practice spanning Mumbai and London. She completed her Specialist Training Programme at the London Deanery, achieving CCT — the gold standard UK qualification — and holds FRCOG from the Royal College of Obstetricians & Gynaecologists, London.

She holds a European University Diploma in Gynaecological Operative Endoscopy from the University of Auvergne, France, and has trained in da Vinci Robotic Surgery at UCL, London. Her practice at Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital attracts patients from across India and internationally for high risk obstetrics, complex gynaecological surgery, and women's health.
